Full SAT Test

Complete SAT practice test that simulates the official exam experience.

  • 98 questions total
  • 134 minutes to complete
  • Includes all sections: Reading, Writing, and Math
  • Most questions are multiple-choice format
  • Scoring aligned with official SAT scale (400-1600)

Rapid Assessment

Quick diagnostic test to identify your strengths and areas for improvement.

  • 30 questions selected from all sections
  • 45 minutes to complete
  • Provides immediate analysis of performance by topic
  • Great for tracking progress between full practice tests

SAT Math

Focus exclusively on the mathematics portion of the SAT.

  • 44 questions total
  • 70 minutes to complete
  • Covers algebra, problem-solving, data analysis, and advanced math
  • Includes both calculator and no-calculator sections

SAT Writing

Practice the writing and language section of the SAT.

  • 25 questions total
  • 35 minutes to complete
  • Tests grammar, punctuation, and rhetorical skills
  • Passage-based questions focusing on editing and improving text

SAT Reading

Focus on improving your reading comprehension skills for the SAT.

  • 30 questions total
  • 40 minutes to complete
  • Includes various passage types: literature, historical documents, science, and social studies
  • Tests comprehension, analysis, and evidence-based reasoning

SAT Essay

Practice writing a structured analytical essay based on a provided passage.

  • 50 minutes to complete
  • Analyze an author's argument and explain how they build their case
  • Scored on reading, analysis, and writing skills
  • Optional but recommended for college applications
